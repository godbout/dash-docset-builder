The Field

All is calm
I'm lying there
The sun warming my face
The wind
Blows the barley
Pushes the clouds
The sky is so blue
I have to squeeze my eyes
I'm alone
The senses
On my skin
On my arms
I'm alive
I can feel
I am nature
I am life
And even if
I hurt
I cannot die
By my own hand
Yet
